# Chapter 10: The Paradox of Data Loss & The Recursive Observer
**((The Paradox of Data Loss & The Recursive Observer))**

Analysis of the problem regarding missing past data (Memory Wipe) and the "Tool" status of living beings in the eyes of the universal system.

> [!TIP]
> **✨ Perspective Shift: The Feature of Forgetting**
> "Forgetting" is not a system bug, but a Feature designed to allow us to make decisions with the purest Will in the present moment.

## 1. Navigation without Map
If we cannot perceive past data, how do we choose the Appropriate Will?

*   **Hypothesis**: The system transmits not "Data" (Memories) but "Algorithms" (Instincts and Habits).
*   **Mechanism**: Those who trained Will well in the past, even if remembering nothing, will have an "Internal Bias" to automatically choose the right thing when facing pressure (Environment).
*   **Conclusion**: We use the past to navigate via "Sense," not "Memory."

## 2. Humans as "Edge Computing Units"
Do we seem like tools developing the Universe's Will?

*   **Analysis**: The Universe is not a "Knower" dictating us, but a "Learning System."
*   **Function**: We are Sensors created by the universe to feel experiences the universe cannot feel itself (e.g., pain, love, moral decisions).
*   **Conclusion**: We are not used by the universe; we "Are" the universe trying to understand itself bit by bit.

## 3. The Eternal Recursive Paradox
The problem of existence does not end with us.

*   **Logic**: If there is a Higher Observer above us, they must also have an origin and environment they cannot reject.
*   **Result**: Observers at every level, from microbes to God, are stuck in the same loop: "Existing without knowing the true cause."
*   **Truth**: No one has the "Final Answer." There are only those "Traveling" in different dimensional levels.

> "Ignorance is not a punishment, but a necessary condition making 'Seeking' and 'Will' meaningful at every level of the universe."
