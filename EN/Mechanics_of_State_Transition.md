## 1. The Transformation Mechanism
We do not "step" into the future clockwise, but are "Re-generated" in every mind-moment by our own Will.

### 1.1 The State Equation
Viewing the Will owner as a "System Node," their future state is not random but the result of a function:

$$S_{t+1} = S_t + \Delta(W \times A)$$

*   **$S_t$ (Current State)**: Current level (intellect, mind resolution, logic structure).
*   **$W$ (Will)**: Intensity and direction of Will (e.g., aiming for truth vs. illusion).
*   **$A$ (Action)**: Actions taken (physical and mental).
*   **$S_{t+1}$ (Future State)**: New state of the Will owner.

**Mechanism Operation:**
*   **Overwriting**: Every time Will operates, it doesn't just affect the exterior but "Overwrites Code" into the memory and neural structure of the doer.
*   **Momentum Accumulation**: If Will focuses on Clarity repeatedly, the system builds a "Neural Pathway," making future access to truth faster and less energy-consuming.

### 1.2 Probability Locking
At the quantum level, the future is a Cloud of Probability.
*   **Mechanism**: Will acts as an Observer making a "Selection."
*   **Result**: Once selected, other paths Collapse, and the chosen path becomes the "Permanent Structure" of the future self.
*   **Example**: Choosing to "Doubt to find truth" now locks the future state as "One with higher intellect," discarding the chance of becoming "One who is ignorant."

*   The owner feels the world has changed, not because the world changed, but because the "Receiver" (themself) has tuned to a new frequency.
