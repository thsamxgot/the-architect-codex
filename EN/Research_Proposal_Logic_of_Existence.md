# Concept Research Proposal: The Architecture of Rational Mechanism and Irrational Existence

## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Background and Significance
Humanity attempts to understand the universe through logic and science, adhering to the basic "Principle of Sufficient Reason." However, when logical inquiry proceeds to the ultimate origin or the fundamental reality, we often face a Cognitive Deadlock or Paradox unexplainable by linear logic.

The problem lies not in the universe's imperfection but in the limitations of traditional methodology that cannot distinguish between "Mechanism" and "Existence," leading to attempts to force reason upon something that by nature exists beyond reason.

### 1.2 Approach and Scope
This study proposes a "High-Resolution Mental Simulation" methodology as a laboratory to dissect components of reality, focusing on analyzing structural relationships between:
*   **Will**: The Observer and Driver.
*   **Mechanism**: The universal operating procedure (Input-Process-Output).
*   **Root**: The origin requiring no explanation.

## 2. Research Hypotheses
From analysis and mental simulation, core hypotheses for logical proof are:

### Core Hypothesis
> "The universe and all things exist on an Irrational Root, even though their operation is completely controlled by a Rational Mechanism."

### Sub-Hypotheses
*   **Mechanical Relation**: All matter and energy behavior follows Input -> Process -> Output rules, predictable by logic. But these rules are "How," not the "Why" of existence.
*   **init State**: There must be a default start value (init) that "Exists" without prior conditions. This state is outside Causality because it is the point originating cause and effect.
*   **Role of Will**: The Observer's Will is a necessary factor giving the universe systemic meaning. Without perceiving Will, the universe would be merely a Void of Probability.
