# Analysis: The Output of Will
**((Analysis: The Output of Will))**

Why is an individual unavoidably the owner of their Karma (actions)?

## 1. Feedback Loop
Will is like an Operator inputting commands into the system. The Result (Output) always loops back as new Input for the doer.

*   **Summary**: We don't just "receive the result" of action, we "become" the result of that action.

## 2. Signature in the Void
In an empty universe, actions born of Free Will are the only things with "Weight."

*   Once acting, it becomes a "Constant" in the universe equation that cannot be deleted.
*   The result of action is the only proof that the "Observer" truly exists, not just an illusion.

## 3. Systemic Responsibility
Since the universe works with Rational Mechanism, it must "React" to force straightforwardly.

*   If Will sends a "Harmony" frequency, the universe reflects harmony back.
*   If Will sends a "Dissonance" frequency, the universe reflects resistance back. This is the physics of morality working automatically without a judge.
