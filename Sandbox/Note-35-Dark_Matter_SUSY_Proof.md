# Note 35: Dark Matter and SUSY Proof

**Subject:** Mathematical Disproof of Hyperscience
**Reference:** The Architect's Codex - Myth Busting

---

## 1. Dark Matter $\neq$ Particles
*   **Codex Solution:** Space Tension.
*   **Mechanism:** Rotating galaxies twist the local fabric of space.
*   **Math:** The tension ($T$) acts as an attractive force akin to gravity, eliminating the need for WIMPs.

## 2. SUSY $\neq$ Possible
*   **Topology Proof:**
    *   **Fermion:** Tethered Knot (Spin 1/2).
    *   **Boson:** Untethered Twist (Integer Spin).
*   **Conflict:** You cannot remove the "Tether" or "Knot" without cutting the strand (breaking physics). Thus, a Fermion cannot have a Boson superpartner.

## Summary
The universe uses **Occam's Razor**. It adjusts the properties of Space (Tension) rather than inventing invisible particles.
