## 1. Probability of Origin
$$ P(Existence) = \lim_{t \to \infty} (1 - (1 - p)^t) = 1 $$
*   **Meaning:** In infinite time, a Glitch is a **Mathematical Certainty**.

## 2. Calculus of Life
$$ O(t) = \int_{0}^{t} [ E(\tau) + \lambda \cdot W(\tau) ] \,d\tau $$
*   **$E(\tau)$:** Environment/Karma (Fixed).
*   **$W(\tau)$:** Will/Effort (Variable).
*   **Correction:** We can deviate from the karmic path by applying specific Will ($W$).

## 3. Species Filter (Sigmoid)
$$ S(x) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-k(R - R_{th})}} $$
*   **$0$:** Matter (Low Reactivity).
*   **$1$:** Will (High Reactivity).

## 4. Limit of Nirvana
$$ \lim_{Attachment \to 0} Latency = 0 $$
*   **Zero Latency:** Nirvana is the state where Data Mass (Attachment) is zero, resulting in instant processing.

## 5. Suffering Algorithm
$$ Suffering = Pain \times Resistance $$
*   **Solution:** Validates that if Resistance is 0 (Acceptance), Suffering becomes 0 even if Pain exists.
