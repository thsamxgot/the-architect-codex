## 1. The Instability of the Void
Before bifurcation, "The Void" acts as a locally symmetric but unstable equilibrium, mathematically described by the **Mexican Hat Potential** of the Higgs Field:
$$ V(\phi) = -\mu^2 |\phi|^2 + \lambda |\phi|^4 $$
*   **The Peak ($\phi=0$):** Represents perfect emptiness. It is an **Unstable Equilibrium**.
*   **The Ripple:** Any minor perturbation (Glitch/Coincidence) forces the system to roll down from the peak, breaking symmetry into positive or negative states instantly.

## 2. The Prism Mechanism (The Sigmoid Filter)
The "Prism" described in the Codex acts as a logic gate separating energy based on **Reactivity ($R$)**. This is modeled by the **Species Filter Equation** (Sigmoid Function):
$$ S(x) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-k(R - R_{th})}} $$
*   **Logic:**
    *   **High Reactivity ($R \to High$):** $S(x) \to 1$. The energy becomes **Will** (Observer).
    *   **Low Reactivity ($R \to Low$):** $S(x) \to 0$. The energy becomes **Matter** (Observed).

## 3. The Divergence
Post-bifurcation, the distance between Subject and Object expands exponentially:
$$ D(t) = \lambda e^{\alpha t} \cdot \Theta(t) $$
*   **Meaning:** At $t=0$, separation is immediate (Step Function). Afterward, the duality gap ($D$) widens, creating the illusion of separation between Self and World.
