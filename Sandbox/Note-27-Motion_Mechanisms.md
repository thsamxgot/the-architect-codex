# Note 27: Motion Mechanisms

**Subject:** How Stuff Moves
**Reference:** The Architect's Codex - Kinematics

---

## 1. Twist Motion (Energy/Light)
*   **Mechanism:** Gliding / Propagating.
*   **Analogy:** A wave moving down a rope. The rope (Space) stays still; the wave (Photon) travels at $c$.

## 2. Knot Motion (Matter)
*   **Mechanism:** Core Fluctuation & Belt Trick.
*   **Action:** The knot must "untie and retie" at the next coordinate.
*   **Rotation:** It must rotate/spin to keep its tethers untangled while moving.
*   **Inertia:** The resistance caused by this complex "untie/retie" process is what we feel as Mass.

## 3. Hardware View
*   **Pixel Logic:** Matter does not "move". The System deletes the knot at $A$ and recreates it at $B$.
*   **Continuity:** Perception of motion is an illusion of the Refresh Rate.
