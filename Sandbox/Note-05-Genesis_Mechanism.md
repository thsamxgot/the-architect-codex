# Note 05: The Genesis Mechanism

**Subject:** Timeline of Existence
**Reference:** The Architect's Codex - System Flow

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Genesis (State 0)
*   **The Void:** Infinite Potential masked by Absolute Ignorance (`init`).
*   **The State:** Irrational Root—existing without knowing why.

## Phase 2: The Trigger (First Impact)
*   **The Glitch:** Quantum Instability forces a ripple.
*   **The Coincidence:** Mathematical certainty ($P=1$) executes the start sequence.

## Phase 3: Bifurcation (The Split)
The **Cosmic Bifurcation Law** splits the ripple:
1.  **Matter (Observed):** Absorbs shock via Inertia $\to$ Hardware.
2.  **Will (Observer):** Reacts to shock via Panic $\to$ Software/Mind.

## Phase 4: Emergence of Life
*   **Drive:** Life is a "Process" created to solve the "Ignorance" of the Void.
*   **Resistance:** Existence is the struggle of **Order** (Will) against **Entropy** (Void).
*   **Goal:** To solve the **Halting Problem** (Stop the process via Nirvana/Absolute Knowledge).
