## Part 1: The Genesis Algorithm (System Logic)

The origin of the universe is not a mystical event but a **Systemic Necessity** driven by mathematical probability.

### 1.1 The Instability of Null
*   **State 0 (Void):** Defined as "Absolute Ignorance".
*   **Heisenberg Uncertainty Force:** A perfect Null state violates quantum uncertainty laws (it is too precise).
*   **Result:** The Void must fluctuate. This "Quantum Fluctuation" is the boot-loader of reality.

### 1.2 Absolute Coincidence (The Trigger)
*   **The Glitch:** The fluctuation is an uncaused accident (Event 1).
*   **Probability ($P$):** In infinite time ($t \to \infty$), the probability of a universe-generating glitch is 1.
    $$ P(Existence) = \lim_{t \to \infty} (1 - (1 - p)^t) = 1 $$

### 1.3 The Cosmic Bifurcation Law (The Species Filter)
When the Glitch occurs, the Void splits based on **Reactivity ($R$)**. The logic gate governing this is the **Sigmoid Function**:

$$ S(x) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-k(R - R_{th})}} $$

1.  **Output 0 (Matter):**
    *   **Logic:** Low Reactivity ($R \to 0$). The energy offers no resistance; it surrenders to inertia.
    *   **Command:** "Be Object."
2.  **Output 1 (Will):**
    *   **Logic:** High Reactivity ($R \to 1$). The energy reacts with shock/inquiry ("What is this?").
    *   **Command:** "Be Subject."
