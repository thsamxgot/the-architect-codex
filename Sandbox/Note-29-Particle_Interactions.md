# Note 29: Particle Interactions

**Subject:** Collision Mechanics
**Reference:** The Architect's Codex - Quantum Interactions

---

## 1. Collision (Overlap)
*   **Mechanism:** Strands pass through each other ("Phantom" behavior).
*   **Result:** **Reidemeister Move II** (Poke).
*   **Force:** Corresponds to **Weak Force** interactions (e.g., Beta Decay).

## 2. Bonding (Linking)
*   **Mechanism:** Knots become entangled.
*   **Math:** **Linking Number ($Lk$)** changes from 0 to Integer ($1, 2, \dots$).
*   **Example:** Quarks linked by Gluons to form a Proton.

## 3. Hadronization (Separation)
*   **Scenario:** Pulling quarks apart.
*   **Result:** The strand stretches until it snaps.
*   **Repair:** The energy of the snap creates a new **Particle-Antiparticle pair** (Genesis Equation) to seal the break.
*   **Observation:** You can never isolate a Quark; you just create more quarks. `Interaction = Topological Surgery`.
