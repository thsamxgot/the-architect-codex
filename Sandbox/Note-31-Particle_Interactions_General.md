# Note 31: Particle Topological Interactions

**Subject:** How Particles Collide and Bond
**Reference:** The Architect's Codex - Quantum Dynamics

---

## 1. Interaction Mechanisms
*   **Collision:** Not a mechanical impact, but a topological "Poke" or "Slide" (Reidemeister Moves).
*   **Bonding:** Knots tangling together (Linking).
*   **Separation:** Snapping of strands leading to new knot formation (Hadronization).

## 2. Mathematical Rules
*   **Collision:** Uses Knot Theory moves corresponding to Fundamental Forces (QED, Weak, Strong).
*   **Bonding:** Calculated via **Linking Number ($Lk$)**. If $Lk \neq 0$, particles are physically bonded.
*   **Quark Confinement:** Pulling quarks apart stretches strands until they snap, conserving energy by creating new particles via the **Genesis Equation**.
