# Note 03: Physics of the Void

**Subject:** Quantum Fluctuation & Probability
**Reference:** The Architect's Codex - Origin Physics

---

## 1. Heisenberg's Uncertainty Principle
*   **Concept:** Nature forbids a state of "Zero Energy" for a definite time.
*   **Mechanism:** The Void typically fluctuates to maintain non-zero uncertainty. These are **Quantum Fluctuations** (Virtual Particles).
*   **Codex Term:** **"The Glitch"**—a systemic error ensuring the Void is never truly empty.

## 2. Spontaneous Symmetry Breaking
*   **Concept:** Perfect Symmetry is unstable (like a ball on a hill peak).
*   **Mechanism:** The system inevitably "rolls down" to a lower energy state (Mexican Hat Potential), breaking symmetry.
*   **Result:** The emergence of Mass and Energy from nothing.

## 3. Probability in Infinite Time
*   **Equation:**
    $$ P(Existence) = \lim_{t \to \infty} (1 - (1 - p)^t) = 1 $$
*   **Logic:** In an infinite timeline, even an infinitesimal probability ($p \approx 0$) becomes a **Certainty** ($P=1$).
*   **Conclusion:** The Universe was not created by intent, but by the **Inevitability of Emergence**.
