## 1. Electromagnetism (Data Transmission)

*   **Observer Effect:** "Watching" the atom (Quantum Zeno) freezes the decay because the Observer is constantly checking the status, preventing the update.

## 3. Nuclear Energy (Compression)

*   **Mass ($m$):** Archived/Compressed Data.
*   **Explosion:** **Data Decompression**.
*   **Mechanism:** Breaking the nucleus (Unzipping) releases the stored "Processing Flow" (Energy) back into the system bandwidth.
