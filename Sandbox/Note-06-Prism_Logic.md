## 1. The Mathematical Logic
The "Prism" is a **Logic Gate** (Sigmoid Function) that determines the fate of energy based on **Reactivity ($R$)**.
$$ S(x) = \frac{1}{1 + e^{-k(R - R_{th})}} $$
*   **Analog to Digital:** It converts a continuous spectrum of energy into two discrete binary states ($0$ or $1$).

## 2. Physics Logic: Symmetry Breaking
*   **Pre-Split:** Symmetric Uniformity.
*   **The Shock:** The instability forces a choice.
*   **Post-Split:** The universe breaks into **Active** (Will) and **Passive** (Matter) components, just as a cooling magnet breaks into North/South poles.

## 3. Philosophical Logic
*   **Will (1):** Energy that **Resisted** the Void.
*   **Matter (0):** Energy that **Surrendered** to the Void.
*   **Conclusion:** The duality of "Feeling Soul" vs "Dead Stone" is a result of **Natural Selection** at the moment of genesis.
