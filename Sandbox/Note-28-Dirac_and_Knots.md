**Reference:** The Architect's Codex - Advanced Physics

## 1. Matter Motion Equation (Dirac)
$$ i\hbar\gamma^\mu \partial_\mu \psi - m\psi = 0 $$
*   **$\psi$:** The "Dance" of the Tangle Core.
*   **$\gamma^\mu$:** Represents the **720-degree rotation** (Belt Trick) required for motion.
*   **$m$:** The structural drag (Mass).

## 2. Energy Motion Equation (Wave)
$$ \frac{\partial^2 \psi}{\partial t^2} = c^2 \nabla^2 \psi $$
*   **$m=0$:** Untangled twists have no drag term.
*   **Result:** Propagation at $c$.

## 3. Movement Algorithm
To move 1 step, the Universe executes **Reidemeister Moves**:
1.  **Delete:** Untie at $x$.
2.  **Shift:** Transfer topology code to $x+1$.
3.  **Create:** Retie at $x+1$.
