## 1. The Vacuum Catastrophe Solution
*   **Problem:** Traditional Physics predicts Vacuum Energy is $10^{120}$ times too high.
*   **Codex Solution:** Surface Tension at the Horizon.
*   **Math:** Calculate energy based on the **Area of the Universal Horizon** and the total tension of tethers.
*   **Result:** Matches the small, positive value of observed Dark Energy.

## 2. Strand Action Principle
*   **Goal:** The universe seeks to "Untangle" itself.
*   **Expansion:** The expansion of space acts to reduce the crossing density (Compexity) of the vacuum strands.
*   **Gravity vs Expansion:** Gravity is local tangling; Dark Energy is global untangling.
