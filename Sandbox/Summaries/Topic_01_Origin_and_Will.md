    *   **Equation:** $P(\text{Existence}) = \lim_{t \to \infty} (1 - (1-p)^t) = 1$
*   **The Uncaused Cause:** "Absolute Coincidence" is the **Irrational Root** that wakes the system up, transforming "Nothing" into "Something" via statistical force.
